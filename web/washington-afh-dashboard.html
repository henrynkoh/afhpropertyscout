Getting Started - Provider Checklist

Training Requirements

  • Home Care Aide Certification
  • 75-hour orientation
  • CPR/First Aid
  • Food Safety
  • 1,000 hours caregiving experience

Insurance Requirements

  • Commercial general liability
  • Professional liability
  • Minimum aggregate insurance amounts

Inspection Preparation

  • WABO building inspection
  • Door clear width
  • Smoke/CO alarms
  • Evacuation capability
  • Escape windows
  • Water heater temperature

Pro Tip

Document septic/water capacity early in the process. This often determines the maximum number of residents you can serve and affects your business model.
